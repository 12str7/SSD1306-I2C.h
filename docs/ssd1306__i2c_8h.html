§ SSD1306_SA

#define SSD1306_SA   0x3C

SSD1306_SA defines I2C address of LCD display. Please, check your device. If you LCD device has different address, change it here. Write command will be SSD1306_SA<<1 and read will be SSD1306_SA<<1 | 1
